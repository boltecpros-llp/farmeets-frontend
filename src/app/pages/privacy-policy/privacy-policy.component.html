The Farmeets Privacy Policy ("Privacy Policy") informs you about our policies and practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information and other data submitted through Farmeets (the "Site"). By using our Services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

  1. Information We Collect
    • Personal Information You Provide: First name, last name, mobile number, email, profile photo, gender, date of birth, languages, and crop interests.
    • Information from Social Networks: If connected, we may collect usernames and profile data.
    • Technical Information: IP address.
  2. How We Use Your Information
    • To create and manage user accounts.
    • To provide customer support and improve user experience.
    • To send updates, alerts, and promotional messages (with consent).
    • To comply with legal obligations and ensure user safety.
  3. Sharing of Information
    • We do not sell or rent user data. Information may be shared with:
    • Service Providers: Third-party vendors assisting our operations.
    • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect safety and rights.
  4. Data Retention and Deletion
    • User data is retained as long as necessary for service functionality and legal compliance.
    • You can request deletion by emailing privacy@farmeets.com.
      • You must provide the registered mobile number linked to your account.
      • We process deletion requests within 10 working days.
    • Account deletion via app:
      • Login → Profile Image → Delete My Account
      • Login → Profile Image → My Profile → Delete my profile
    • Direct deletion link: https://farmeets.com/account-delete
    • All personal data, posts, comments, and associated information will be permanently deleted and cannot be restored.
  5. User Rights and Choices
    • Access, update, or delete personal data.
    • Opt out of marketing communications.
    • Revoke access granted to third-party linked services.
  6. Data Security

    We use industry standard security measures to safeguard user data, but no method is 100% secure.

  7. Compliance with GDPR & CCPA
    • GDPR: EU users have rights to access, correct, or delete their data, and withdraw consent.
    • CCPA: California residents can request information about data collection and deletion.
  8. Children's Privacy
    • Users under 18 are not permitted to use Farmeets.
    • We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
  9. Updates to This Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Major changes will be communicated to users.

  10. Contact Us

    For any questions, contact us at privacy@farmeets.com or visit www.farmeets.com.
    By using our app, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
